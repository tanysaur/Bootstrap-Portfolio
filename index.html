About Me

Howdy! I'm Alyssa but I go by my nickname, Ysa (pronounced Ee-suh). I am a network engineer during the day, and study to become a developer at night. When I am not rescuing our customers from dropped calls and low data speeds, I practice coding with hopes of making a difference in the world one line of code at a time. I am interested about the development of IoT devices and how this can further improve the human condition. Growing up in a developing country as well as traveling the world grounds me and inspires me to create solutions to the community's problems. I hope that some day I see my product come to fruition, may it be in the form of software, hardware or both.
