About Me

Howdy! I'm Alyssa but I go by Ysa (pronounced Ee-suh). I am a network engineer during the day, and studies to become a developer at night. When I am not rescuing our customers from dropped calls and low data speeds, I practice coding with hopes of making a difference in the world one line of code at a time. I am in the journey of learning more about the development of IoT devices and how this can further improve the human condition. Visiting developing countries grounds me and inspires me to create solutions to the community's problems.
